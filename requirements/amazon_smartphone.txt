UserStory: Purchase a smartphone under 30000 on Amazon by applying filters for price, ratings, and reviews.
Description: As a price-conscious shopper, I want to find and purchase a smartphone under 30000 on Amazon by filtering products based on price, ratings, and reviews, so that I can compare top-rated options within my budget and make a confident purchase.
AcceptanceCriteria1: Given I am on the Amazon homepage, When I navigate to Mobiles - Smartphones, Then I should see the smartphone listings page.
AcceptanceCriteria2: Given I am on the Smartphones listing page, When I apply a price filter of Up to 30000 or enter Max Price 30000, Then only smartphones priced at or below 30000 are shown.
AcceptanceCriteria3: Given the price filter is applied, When the results reload, Then the number of products updates accordingly.
AcceptanceCriteria4: Given filtered products are displayed, Then each product has a listed price greater then equal to 30000 (before coupons).
